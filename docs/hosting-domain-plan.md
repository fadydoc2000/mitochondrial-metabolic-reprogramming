# Hosting and Domain Plan for Online Gaming Accessories Store

## Hosting Requirements

To run a WordPress/WooCommerce store effectively, the hosting environment should meet the following minimum specifications:

- **PHP**: Version 7.4 or higher
- **MySQL**: Version 5.6 or higher (or MariaDB equivalent)
- **HTTPS**: SSL certificate support (essential for secure payments and customer trust)
- **Storage**: Adequate space for product images, database, and backups (minimum 10 GB SSD recommended)
- **Bandwidth**: Sufficient for expected traffic (unmetered or high monthly transfer preferred)
- **Performance**: Optimized for WordPress (PHP OPcache, caching mechanisms)
- **Support**: Reliable customer support (24/7 preferred)
- **Uptime**: Guarantee of 99.9% or higher
- **Backups**: Automated backup solutions (daily preferred)

## Domain Name Suggestions

For a gaming accessories store targeting the general public, consider domain names that are:
- Easy to remember and spell
- Relevant to gaming or accessories
- Preferably .com extension (most trusted and recognizable)

### Suggested Domain Names:
1. GameGearHub.com
2. PixelPlayAccessories.com
3. ControllerCrush.com
4. GamingGearDepot.com
5. GearUpGaming.com
6. PlayStyleStore.com
7. ControllerCastle.com
8. GamingAddons.com
9. JoyfulJoysticks.com
10. EpicGearEmporium.com

### Tips for Choosing a Domain:
- Keep it short and memorable (under 15 characters ideal)
- Avoid numbers and hyphens if possible
- Consider using keywords like "game", "gear", "play", "controller", "gaming"
- Check availability on domain registrars (Namecheap, GoDaddy, Google Domains)
- Register for multiple years if possible to secure the domain and often get better rates

## Next Steps

1. **Select a Hosting Provider**: Compare the options above based on budget and technical needs.
2. **Choose a Domain Name**: Select from the suggestions or brainstorm your own, then check availability.
3. **Purchase Hosting and Domain**: Many providers offer a free domain for the first year when purchasing annual hosting.
4. **Point Domain to Hosting**: Update DNS settings to point your domain to the hosting server.
5. **Install WordPress**: Use the host's one-click installer or manual method.
6. **Proceed with WooCommerce Installation**: Refer to the WooCommerce setup guide.
